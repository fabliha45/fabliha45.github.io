Price and Product Comparison

The "Price and Product Comparison" dashboard provides a panoramic view of the skincare industry and how it works to bridge the gap between drugstore and high-end products. It visualizes price distribution across categories like serums, sunscreens, and cleansers, analyzing ingredient similarity among products with different price limits. Even though the data is limited but audience can visualize whether the higher costs are associated with better results and further understand by product category to find options that best fit one needs helps people make better decisions based on value and quality.

Ingredients Comparison

The "Ingredients Comparison" dashboard allows for a comprehensive analysis of skincare products, focusing on what really matters: the ingredients. Here with the dropdown option one can select which product they would like to compare whether a face wash, makeup remover or a serum. It shows the efficiency of active components like retinol, hyaluronic acid, and niacinamide—present in both drugstore and high-end products—and points out by highlighting ingredient overlap how affordable options can compete with luxury brands. On top of that, it allows for customization in order to find products that target very specific concerns, be it hydration, anti-aging, or acne. Equip yourself with this tool to make informed, evidence-based skincare choices and cut through the noise created by branding and marketing.

Budget Set

The "Budget Set" dashboard is your go-to guide in creating a skincare routine that balances quality and affordability perfectly. The purpose of this is mainly for personal use so it becomes accessible for every audience to have a quality skincare by their choice. It gives personalized recommendations based on specific skin issues, be it acne, dryness, or even anti-aging along with cost-efficient recommendations across a variety of price ranges, helping you discover products that deliver exceptional value without compromising results. By recognizing routine essentials, it simplifies each step of your regimen—from cleansing to treatment—with effective, budget-friendly options. The dashboard will even help you budget in such a way that you can spend money wisely to get the best out of your budget.
