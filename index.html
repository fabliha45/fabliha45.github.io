Beauty Comes with a Price: But How Much is it Worth?

Welcome to an in-depth exploration of the skincare industry. This project compares high-end luxury skincare products with affordable drugstore options to answer a critical question: Are premium prices justified by superior quality, or are we just paying for branding?

Discover the Insights

The global skincare market is thriving, projected to grow from $104.24 billion in 2022 to $145.82 billion by 2029. Despite this growth, consumers face a dilemma: choosing between luxury products and affordable alternatives. This project investigates whether high-end products truly offer superior quality or if consumers are simply paying for prestige.
